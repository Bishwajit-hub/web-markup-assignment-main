Explore the Van Gogh Museum

The Van Gogh Museum in Amsterdam is located at **Museumplein 6, 1071 DJ Amsterdam**, in the heart of the city's Museum Quarter. ### Opening Hours: - **Daily:** 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM - **Fridays and Saturdays:** Extended hours until 10:00 PM - **Holiday hours:** May vary, so it's best to check before visiting. ### Ticket Prices (2024): - **Adults (18+):** €20 – €22 - **Youth (under 18):** Free - **Students:** Discount available with valid student ID - **Museumkaart holders:** Free entry It's recommended to book tickets online in advance due to high demand.

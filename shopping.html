Welcome to Dutch Local Market

Amsterdam's largest local Dutch market, Albert Cuyp Market, is located in the De Pijp neighborhood along Albert Cuypstraat. It operates Monday to Saturday, from 9:30 AM to 5:00 PM, offering a wide variety of goods, including fresh produce, clothes, and street food.

Amsterdam's floating flower market, **Bloemenmarkt**, is open Monday to Saturday from 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM, and on Sundays from 11:00 AM to 5:30 PM. It is located along the Singel Canal between Muntplein and Koningsplein. This iconic market offers a wide variety of flowers and souvenirs.
